# Red Sun RP
The main repository for the Red Sun RP.

## About It
The Red Sun RP is a RP that takes place in a time when Sol is at its RG phase at about 6,500,000,000 years from now.

## Orbital Terms
* L_O: The generalization of a low planetary orbit.
* H_O: The generalization of high planetary orbit.
* GEO: Geostationary orbit.
* VH_O: Generalization of a very high planetary orbit.
* ESOI: A term for the edge of a planet's SOI, or sphere of influence.

*For the blanks in the generalizations, fill in the blank with the first initial of the planet you're talking about.*
